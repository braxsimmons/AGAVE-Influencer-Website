About

How to Play

How does this contest work? It’s simple!

1. Play for a prize by hitting the circular button on the bottom right-hand corner of the console.

2. Keep hitting the button until "your hit number" matches the "next winning number".

3. You may notice the "HIT NUMBER" increases by more than 1 each time you hit the button. This is because other users are also hitting that button. You are in a race against your fellow users to land on the "NEXT WINNING NUMBER"! Keep hitting!

4. You'll see over 20 winning numbers each day in the "Next Winning Numbers" box. Each winning number corresponds to one of the four prizes available. You won’t know which prize it is until you land on the Next Winning Number. The quantities of each prize type per contest will always be disclosed at the bottom of the contest module.

5. In the center of the screen you'll see the image of 4 prizes that are displayed in a slider. You may swipe to see each prize.

6. Unlock higher value prizes by clicking on the prize image and performing simple tasks. (Don't worry, your personal information is never sold or spammed.)

7. Your odds never decrease. As more players play more prizes are added!

8. Winning prizes will be sent electronically to the email address provided.

9. Click on “Previous Winners” in your account tab to see previous winners.

10. Have Fun!
